## Ancient Barrow

  
==Overview of Ancient Barrow==

==In-role description of area==

In the long-lost days of Antioch's founding, the people who inhabited the Shaahri desert were distinctly pacifist. Still, they had to deal with their enemies somehow, and threats did indeed come upon them in the desert. So, they built a barrow in the hills of the Graytem and there they confined those who might cause harm. The barrow is watched over by Tanari Guardians pledged to keep the dangers of the barrow confined, and sworn to the cause of Peace. It serves as a prison for the mutated children who were the original Akrabi, born to the people of the desert, who looked upon them with horror, as well as some of the ancient Stavennites who chased after the followers of Ardor Baely Natan and Yarl Din. A group of insane furies also inhabit the barrow, locked away in their guarded prison for the crime of rebellion. Few know what threat lies in the westernmost room of the Barrow, for it is that cage which is guarded most fiercely.

==OOC area description==

This room is to flesh out some history and explain how the pacifists who founded Antioch managed, what happened to the Akrabi kids who so disgusted the founders, and go into more detail about the barrow in the Graytems. It'll be a bashing/questing area, roughly 59 rooms. It should be good XP for a level 70ish person.

== Help File ==  
  
Sunny Latonin Island, where whispered rumor insists treasure has been  
buried, is a lovely island with two sandy beaches and exotic flora and  
fauna. The plateau from which it takes its name gives a glorious view  
of the island and the sea around it, and also serves as a nesting  
ground for the rare, black-footed albatross. More than one ship has

-- and to choose a wise captain to deliver you.  
  
== OOC Information ==  
  
"Latonin Island" will be roughly 68 rooms. It's a large island. The  
southeastern and northeastern portions will be beach. The western line  
of rooms will be cliffs. Most of the inner island rooms will be  
forest, though the middle "circle" I plan to have be a plateau. There  
will be two "groups" of pirates, one located on the northeast beach,  
led by the captain, the other at the southeastern beach and led by the  
first mate. Other pirates will be scattered around the inner island,  
looking for food to feed the crew with while they search for the  
treasure that they think was buried here by the pirates of Takum. On  
the western side, there will be a shipwrecked sailor who you can talk  
to to get information about how to actually find the buried treasure  
if you give him a tinderbox so that he can light a signal fire. There  
is also an aggressive colony of nesting albatross, and you can help  
the pirates try to protect their boat from the termites on the island.
