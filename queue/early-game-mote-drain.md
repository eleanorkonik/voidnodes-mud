# Early Game Mote Drain from Population

The early game should have a constant drain on motes from the number of people it has to support. Even though there are a certain number of beds, the number of rooms and people have a cost for the world seed to keep alive and safe, keep the air filtered, etc.

Once we hit a different stage of growth it can be more self-sustaining as the stories start adding motes and it becomes self-generating. But in the beginning we need to feed it.
