# Inventory Slots System

Slots that can be filled for the explorer — can only bring home a certain amount of things from each zone.

Starting capacity: one large item, two medium items, and 20 small items.

Artifacts can extend this (e.g., free to carry all tools, increase large item capacity). Strength checks for large items, dexterity for small, with rapidly increasing difficulty checks.

Slots are free, extras require stat checks.
